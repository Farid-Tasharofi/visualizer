Why 50 location classes but only 29 observed positive in batch 1

21/50 location classes have zero positive cases in this 98-case release — a long-tail imbalance, not a labeling gap (the full 50-class taxonomy is defined challenge-wide; batch 1 is simply too small to cover every rare site). See the full per-class table below.
